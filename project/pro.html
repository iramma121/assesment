                      About us

view of the need for systematic planning and to control and regulate the developments in and around Doddaballapura city, the Karnataka State Government intends to create Doddaballapura Local Planning Area.For this purpose 257 villages of BIAAPA and 37 villages of Nelamangala Planning Authority were taken away from the jurisdiction of BIAAPA and Nelamangala Planning Authority as per Section 4B of Karnataka Town & Country Planning Act 1961.vide notification No: Na AaE/95/BMR/2016,Dated: 30-11-2017.(Notification-I copy) The Doddaballapura Planning Authority has been constituted vide Government Notification No: NaAaE-95-BMR-2016 dated 20.03.2018, under section 4(c) of the Karnataka Town & Country Planning Act, 1961. The Government in Notification No: NaAaE-95-BMR-2016 dated: 20.03.2018 has appointed the Metropolitan Commissioner, Bangalore Metropolitan Region Development Authority, Bangalore as the Chairman of the Authority and Joint Director of Town & Country Planning as the Member-Secretary and Municipal Commissioner, CMC, Doddaballapura has been appointed as the Member of the Authority.
